🎙️ VOICE‑OVER TONE GUIDE
For the 6–8 minute flagship video
1. Tone Personality
The narrator must sound like:

Global authority (Apple‑launch confidence)

African warmth (deep, grounded, human)

Technological precision (clear, sharp, intelligent)

Inspirational leadership (TED‑Talk energy)

Zero arrogance, maximum conviction

Think:
A calm, powerful voice that knows the future is already here.

2. Vocal Qualities
Warm Resonance
Deep but not heavy

Smooth, steady, controlled

Slight African undertone — not accent, presence

Measured Pace
Slow in emotional sections

Medium in technical sections

Fast only during “breakthrough” moments

Emphasis Strategy
Emphasize verbs: build, transform, accelerate, unlock

Emphasize contrasts: not months… but one hour

Emphasize South Africa: For the first time… in the world… South Africa leads.

Breathing
Natural pauses after big statements

Let the words “land”

Silence is part of the message

3. Emotional Arc
Act 1 — History & Struggle
Tone: reflective, respectful, grounded.

Act 2 — The Challenge
Tone: serious, urgent, factual.

Act 3 — The Breakthrough
Tone: rising energy, controlled excitement.

Act 4 — The Technology
Tone: precise, confident, authoritative.

Act 5 — The Impact
Tone: warm, hopeful, inspiring.

Act 6 — The Future
Tone: triumphant, visionary, proud.

4. Forbidden Tones
No hype

No shouting

No sales‑pitch energy

No “corporate jargon voice”

No artificial excitement

This must feel inevitable, not “advertised”.

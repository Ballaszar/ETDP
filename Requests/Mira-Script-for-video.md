## Visual and Editorial Standards
- Subtitle style: sentence case, max 2 lines, high contrast, safe margins for mobile.
- Transition style: slow cross-dissolves between narrative scenes; direct cuts for app workflow moments.
- Camera pace: avoid rapid cuts; keep each major shot on screen long enough to read labels.
- UI capture rule: show complete page header and workflow indicators when demonstrating steps.
- Color consistency: use your app green/blue palette for overlays and lower thirds.

## Audio Mix Guide
- Narration level target: -16 LUFS integrated (stream-safe).
- Music bed: keep 12-18 dB below narrator; dip music further during technical lists.
- Add subtle rise at Scene 3 and Scene 10 for emotional lift.
- Avoid hard limiter pumping; preserve vocal warmth and clarity.
